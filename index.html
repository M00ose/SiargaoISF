About Us

The Siargao International Surfing Festival is one of the biggest surfing events in the Philippines where local and foreign surfers show their best in the teardrop-shaped island in Surigao Del Norte, Philippines.

The recent years have been challenging for the island, its people, and its patrons brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic and Typhoon Odette.

However, the strong sense of community didn’t let the spirits of the Surigao-nons down. With the help of LGUs and the private sector, the island is beginning to stand tall as it once was.

After two years, the Siargao International Surfing Festival is finally back, bigger, and better. This year’s celebration is in honor of the Filipinos who experienced the full fury of the typhoon and continue to feel its effects until today.

Presented by San Miguel Corporation, Congressman Bingo Matugas & The Municipality of General Luna, sanctioned by the WSL.
