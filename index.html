Festival Activities

Come together for Siargao International Surfing Festival’s 26th year.

Be greeted by mini food trucks and stalls from Siargao’s most famous bars and restaurants. You’re in for a culinary adventure on this one.

A portion of the profits will be donated to local businesses that were affected by the pandemic and Typhoon Odette. #BangonSiargao

Support local artisans in a flea market-styled bazaar. From handcrafted accessories to paintings and sculptures, you can see and support the Siargao art scene all in one place.

SIARGAO SOUND SESSIONS

General Luna invites you to experience different sounds in different stages. To make it even better, Tanduay will serve free drinks, all in the name of companionship.

To recognize the communal efforts of the Siargao-nons, LGUs, Filipinos, and the people of the world in helping the island recover, we’ll feature recordings of Siargao before and after its most recent challenges.

Siargao International Surfing Festival

The Siargao International Surfing Festival is one of the biggest surfing events in the Philippines where local and foreign surfers show their best in the teardrop-shaped island in Surigao Del Norte, Philippines.

The recent years have been challenging for the island, its people, and its patrons brought about by the COVID-19 pandemic and Typhoon Odette. However, the strong sense of community didn’t let the spirits of the Surigao-nons down. With the help of LGUs and the private sector, the island is beginning to stand tall as it once was.

After two years, the Siargao International Surfing Festival is finally back, bigger, and better. This year’s celebration is in honor of the Filipinos who experienced the full fury of the typhoon and continue to feel its effects until today.
